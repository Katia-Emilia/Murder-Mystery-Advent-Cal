The Case of the Frosted Murder

An Advent calendar is a countdown to Christmas, revealing surprises each day. In this murder mystery Advent calendar, visit daily to uncover new clues. Piece them together to solve the case and identify the killer before the final reveal on December 25th!

In the quiet town of Winter Hollow, nestled deep within a snow-covered valley, the annual Winter Ball turned dark when the beloved philanthropist and business mogul, Eleanor Frost, was found dead in her mansion on December 1st. With a house full of guests, a tangled web of secrets, and the clock ticking toward Christmas, it's up to you, the detective, to unravel the mystery and unmask the killer.

Main Characters
1.Victor Hale: Eleanor's estranged brother, known for his temper and financial troubles.
2.Martha Green: The Frost family housekeeper, loyal but harboring a mysterious past.
3.Dr. Lila Moore: Eleanor's personal physician, who recently had heated arguments with her.
4.Jonathan Price: A close friend of Eleanor, rumored to have been in love with her.
5.Amelia Frost: Eleanor's ambitious niece, next in line to inherit her fortune.
